# Workforce Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the workforce, including key metrics such as attrition count, attrition rate, active employees, average age, and job satisfaction. Additionally, it offers a detailed breakdown of attrition by gender, age group, department, and education field.

## Key Benefits

- **Data-Driven Insights**: Supports informed decision-making by highlighting workforce trends.
- **Attrition Prediction**: Assists in forecasting future attrition rates and identifying potential areas of concern.
- **Resource Optimization**: Helps in optimizing resources and improving job satisfaction.
- **Diversity Promotion**: Offers a detailed view to tailor HR strategies and promote a diverse and inclusive work environment.

Monitoring attrition is crucial for HR, as it aids in identifying potential issues, minimizing talent loss, and maintaining a stable and satisfied workforce.

## How to Access the Dashboard

1. Click on the file with the `.pbix` extension.
2. Select "View raw" or the "Download" button to download the PBIX file.
